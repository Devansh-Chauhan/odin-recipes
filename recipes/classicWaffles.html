ClassicWaffles

Ingridients

  • All Purpose Flour / Maida - 1 cup
  • Salt- ½ tsp
  • Baking Powder - 2 tsp
  • Sugar - 1 tblsp
  • Egg - 1
  • Milk - ¾ cup
  • Butter - 2 tblsp melted or use oil
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt together. Take egg, milk, oil and vanilla in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Pour this into dry ingredients and mix well.
  3. heat waffle iron and coat with oil.
  4. Pour batter and cook till golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve.
